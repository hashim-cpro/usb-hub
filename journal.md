# July 18th: Started with the schematics and PCB

I finished this whole project basically in a single session since it is a small and straight forward project made by following a tutorial. I started with the schematics and then PCB. But when i switched to PCB i didn't realized that lookout had another chock and was paused in the background. i continued for a hour or so and it was still paused in the background. (yk ts lowkey hurts) in that time i imported a batman board outline and places the components on the board according to it but it was too tight and also realized that i had places the nets in the schematics kinda wrong so went back and fixed those. tried to line everything up for the batman outline but was too tight. ended up ditching it and then just googled just a heart shape(terrible choice for case design later on) and it was at this time that i realized that lookout was paused and went back and resumed it. But anyway i went back and continued with the new board outline and placed the components and routed the board. I was done with the PCB and then i just fired up my favourite CAD program(shapr 3d, i have liberated it from the greedy apple store who want me to pay 50$/month for it on my ipad 🏴‍☠️) but i think that was the most fun part out of all
